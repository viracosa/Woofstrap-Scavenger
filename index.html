Our mission

We are the Girls Team of The Avengers. The Avengers were a team of extraordinary individuals, with either superpowers or other special characteristics. Our purpose was to protect global stability from inner or extraterrestrial threats. Though primarily affiliated with the United States of America, we worked with the peaceful interests of the whole world in mind rather than a specific country or organization.
